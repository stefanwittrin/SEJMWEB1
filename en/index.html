What is SEJM?

SEJM guides managers and leaders to become the best version of themselves – without losing themselves along the way. The starting point is always the individual: your self-awareness, your strengths, and your ability to achieve results while maintaining well-being.

With more than 20 years of experience as a manager and leader, combined with certified coaching and a licence for Extended DISC, SEJM creates tailored solutions for individuals, groups and organisations across all industries.
